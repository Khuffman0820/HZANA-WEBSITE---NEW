Getting Started

Four steps to stillness

Sessions can be as short as a few minutes, or extended into longer periods of rest. There is no right way — only what feels aligned.

01
Power On

Press and hold the power button. Hzana remembers your last frequency and settings automatically.

02
Select a Frequency

Choose from 10 healing frequencies on the back dial. Each has 4 original sound environments to explore.

03
Set Your Light

Hold the light button to activate. Tap to cycle through 7 paired frequency colors and set brightness across 10 levels.

04
Allow Yourself to Settle

Sit or lie comfortably. Begin with a lower volume. Let the sound settle around you. There is no right way.
