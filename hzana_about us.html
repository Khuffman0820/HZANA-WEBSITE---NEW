Our Journey

"Hzana began during a time when we needed it most."

Our first baby became seriously ill at just three months old — RSV and a persistent cough that made it nearly impossible for him to rest. We found ourselves searching for anything that could bring him comfort.

In that season, we discovered the quiet power of healing frequencies. Within less than an hour of playing the sounds, he fell asleep for the first time in days — and slept deeply for hours. It felt like his body could finally relax.

∿

Over the next few days, his breathing became calmer. More natural again. In the middle of fear and exhaustion, those same sounds became a daily anchor — bringing a sense of calm back into our home.

In the years that followed, we returned to frequency healing again and again — whenever we were sick, overwhelmed, or simply needed to reset. But it wasn't until the birth of our second child that we began searching for a device that could bring these frequencies into our home in a simple, intentional way.

∿

It didn't exist.
So we created Hzana.

A device designed not just for sleep — but for stillness, restoration, and connection. As a reflection of where it all began, we included a frequency designed to support breath and relaxation — an homage to the moment that started it all.

What began as something for our children became something for us — and now, for you.
